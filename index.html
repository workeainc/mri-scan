No, you don’t need to pay in advance. You can pay directly at the diagnostic centre through UPI, cash, or card.

True Report is a direct-to-customer platform. We have special tie-ups with partner diagnostic centres, and we pass these negotiated rates directly to our customers—ensuring the lowest possible prices.
